Stress and anxiety are common problems for high schoolers. Whether the stress comes from starting a new school, tests, homework, or social pressure all these things can make performace and happiness levels worse. Luckily, there are many simple ways to try and relieve stress, like breathing excersises or visualization techniques.

Simple Methods for Stress Relief

  • Box Breathing
    • Step 1: Breathe in through your nose for four seconds
    Step 2: Hold for four seconds
    Step 3: Breathe out through your mouth for four seconds
    Step 4: Hold for four seconds
  • 4-7-8 Breathing Exercise
    • Step 1: Breathe in through your nose for four seconds
    Step 2: Hold for seven seconds
    Step 3: Breathe out through your mouth for eight seconds
