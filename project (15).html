About This Website:

    This website was created to help spread awearness and knowledge about mental health and mental well-being. This website will help give you a better understanding about what mental health and mental well-being is and how you can help maintain a healthy mental state and how to deal with mental health problems for yourself or other people.

About Mental Health:

    Mental health is a person's mental, social, and physical well-being. Mental health effects your involvement in your daily functions and activies. It can affect how well you do in school, it can affect your relationship with your family and other people, it can also affect how you proform your everyday activities and life functions, and much more.

Warning Signs on if Someone Has a Mental Health Problem:

  • Feeling excessively depressed or low

  • Abuse of substance use like drugs or alcohol

  • Avoiding family and friends everyday

  • Extreme mood changes, where someone is extremely happy at one moment but then is deeply depressed the next

  • Prolonged or strong feelings of irritability or anger

  • Inability to cary out daily activites or handle daily problems and stress

  • Change in eating habits and sleeping habits

  • Change in school performance
